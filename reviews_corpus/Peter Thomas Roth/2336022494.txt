I was gifted the Peter Thomas Roth Water Drench Hyaluronic Cloud Rich Barrier Moisturizer in exchange for my honest review. In short: I didn’t love it, but that’s because of my own personal preferences. The cream did moisturize my dry skin and left it feeling hydrated all day, and the dimethicone in the cream did keep all the moisture in by creating a barrier (which has been great, especially considering the temperatures lately). However, I personally don’t like the feel of dimethicone on my face -- although it is silky and smooth, I prefer other occlusive ingredients. If dimethicone is your jam, though, then you’ll love it!
