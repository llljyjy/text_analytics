I love this oil and love how versatile it is. I usually mix a couple drops in with my moisturizer in the morning as part of my daily routine. If my skin needs a midday boost, I just put a few drops in my hand and press it in. It’s so hydrating and is very light on my skin and doesn’t leave behind any tackiness or reside it just absorbs beautifully. My only complaint is the dropper. Its super sensitive. Sometimes I just lift the dropper out of the bottle and if my hands not there to catch it, the oil starts dripping onto my counter even if I’m not squeezing the dropper. Other than that the product itself is great. The hydrating boost my skin needed
