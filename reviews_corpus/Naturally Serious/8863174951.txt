What a nice mask for dry skin that needs a bit of clarifying! This is an easy to use and easy to remove mask that contains calming and soothing ingredients such as colloidal oats and NMF paired with clay ingredients. It is not a drying clay mask, however, as hydration is the main focus. I left the mask on for 10 minutes and felt no drying whatsoever, but pushing it longer than 20 minutes (which is not instructed) did feel a smidge drying. So I would say to follow the directions on this one.Upon removal, my skin felt nourished, soft, and with no signs of irritation.I’m quite pleased, and it’s nice to see this more affordable direction from June Jacobs, which has historically been a bit more expensive (and yet a classic) brand.
