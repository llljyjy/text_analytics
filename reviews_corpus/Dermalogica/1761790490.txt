I’ve been a user of the original daily microfoliant for years now and thought I’d try this one out. This product is a lot softer and more gentle. When you lather this up it is very creamy and moisturizing. Unfortunately it doesn’t cut it as an exfoliator; there’s not enough grit for my liking. It may be ideal for someone with very sensitive skin and looking for (super) gentle exfoliation. Sensitive skin exfoliator
