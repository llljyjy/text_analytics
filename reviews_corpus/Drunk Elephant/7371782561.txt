I’m lucky enough to have been able to use this entire line and one point or another and this eye cream doesn’t disappoint. I like the bottle that it turns to open. It’s thick (not too thick) and a little goes a long way. It doesn’t irritate my eyes which is a plus! (I have sensitive eyes). My only complaint is that it does seem to take a while to absorb, so for me this is a night time cream. It’s nice
