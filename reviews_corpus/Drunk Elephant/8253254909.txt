I’m actually pretty impressed with this eye balm. My experience with balms is that they are like in a stick or something and hard to apply. This is actually more like a heavy cream, so you can spread it around the eye area. The area felt immediately refreshed upon application. It’s very hydrating as well. Because of the “cooling” effect, I’ve been using it at night for the past several days. The packaging is cute and functional, you twist the top to get the pump out, and then twist it back inside after using it. Creamy and refreshing
