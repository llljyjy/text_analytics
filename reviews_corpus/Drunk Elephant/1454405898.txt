This serum is too creamy feeling for what I wanted. Plan was to use it on the sun damage on my cheeks, after my hyaluronic acid serum, and possibly before or after my vitamin C serum, then to follow with an all over moisturizer. It might work as an all-over-the-face single use product, but I don’t care for it as a serum. Still, the ingredients list looks really good, so I’m still giving it four stars. On the heavy side for a serum
