I was surprised by this product! A lot of times eye creams are simply repackaged facial moisturizers with a higher price tag, but this doesn’t seem to be the case. The product is a *very* rich cream. You need very little of this. I use only a fraction of a pump and it’s plenty for both eyes. It is very hydrating and sinks in pretty quickly. The hydration lasts all day, but isn’t greasy, and it doesn’t lead to concealer creasing. The fact that it’s in an airless pump bottle is great, and it’s travel friendly since the pump part retracts. I recommend this nan
