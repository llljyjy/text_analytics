Fresh’s sugar lip treatment in papaya (kindly gifted to me by Fresh) is an intensely hydrating lip balm that contains beeswax and a variety of fruit oils (like avocado and lemon peel). It does not seem to contain any actual papaya, so I suppose that’s just the name of the color. The scent is sweet like a sugary lemon wafer. The treatment does contain added fragrance as well as added color, which creates a pretty buildable pink-red on the lips. One note: the tube is luxe and metallic, and the top twists off (so don’t try to pull)! Hydrating and sweetly scented
