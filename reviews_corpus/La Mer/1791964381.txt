This product is supposed to be used after cleansing and provide hydration to the skin. I unfortunately cannot vouch for that. I used it once on the face, neck, and chest and broke out in a really bad rash on my neck and chest. I think that’s because I applied it at night before bed and then added vitamin c on in the morning, so those two ingredients likely reacted poorly together. It may have been user error. The website doesn’t clearly list what the ingredients are so it makes it difficult to know what to pair this with. I’m surprised that this irritated my skin. It’s unfortunate that these are the only key ingredients La Mer lists on its site so I can’t pinpoint what exactly I’m allergic to within the formulation: “Miracle Broth™, The Revival Ferment™, Active Liquid Hydrogel.“ Gave Me a Rash
