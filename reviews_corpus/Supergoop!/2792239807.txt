Based on what the other reviews say about this, yes it is thick, and yes it is like a foundation. BUT if you’re someone like me who has acne scars, hyperpigmentation, and in general just stuff you want to cover, then you’ll love this! I’ve never been into tinted moisturizers because they never give me the coverage I want. But with this I’m getting a product with 50spf AND a decent amount of coverage. I LOVE this product. If you’re expecting something with very very light coverage, this isn’t it. On the thick side but…
