It leaves a glowy and slightly tacky finish. So makeup wears great over this sunscreen. It feels lightweight which is great for summer time. It has a light watermelon scent. I think people who doesn’t like sunscreen scent would really like this! It has a milky consistency, so I could apply this all over my face easily. One big miss for me, it peels bad, especially on hairline and eyebrows. I think it’ll peel bad for people who have lots of peach fuzz all over their face. My eyebrow becomes a mess whenever I put this one. I’m excited to see this sunscreen’s skincare benefits after consistent use. Good overall, but it peels
