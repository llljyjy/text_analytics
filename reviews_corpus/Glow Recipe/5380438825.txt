At first, I was concerned I would break out because this cleanser has oil in it. To my surprise, I did not break out. Instead, I found this to be more hydrating than my other cleansers. It lathers easily and seems to be “creamy“ as opposed to thick. The scent is not strong--neither pleasant nor unpleasant. The skin on my nose tends to be flaky on a daily basis during cold weather, no matter how much I moisturize. I was surprised to notice a dramatic difference after using this cleanser. I would recommend this to anyone who has dry skin. Nourishing & Hydrating
