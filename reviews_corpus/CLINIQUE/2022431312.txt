I wouldn’t say this is my favorite moisturizer I’ve ever tried but it definitely does what it’s supposed to as far as the moisturization. With proper hydration in your skin I feel like wrinkles will subside a bit, which in turn adds to your anti-aging benefits. I do have to say though, it absorbs well on my skin and does not sit on the surface or pill. Now I’m not saying not to try it since everyone’s skin is different but it’s not my favorite and I probably will not repurchase based on my results from consistent use. There is also no fragrance that I can tell which is a plus, because I don’t really want to put something that has artificial fragrance in it if I can at all help it. it's a maybe from me
