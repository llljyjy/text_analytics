I liked this product, didn’t love it. After wearing my skin would feel very soft but it was very heavy. It almost felt like wearing a few layers of full coverage foundation. I kept thinking I needed makeup remover by the end of the day even when I wasn’t wearing makeup. I couldn’t wait to wash it off every day. For background, I’m mid 30s with combination skin. I live in a hot, humid state. If you are in a cooler state or have really dry skin this may be just what you need. Soft skin but heavy
