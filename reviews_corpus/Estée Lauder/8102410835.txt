I received a sample of this product for free. The consistency is quite viscous and reminiscent of k-beauty toners and essences which I really enjoyed. However, I’m not a fan of how much fragrance Ester Lauder consistently puts in their products. There are no skin benefits in fragrance and it is incredibly strong. This product feels nice enough but I don’t feel it’s worth the price, especially considering how similar it feels to k beauty products that are cheaper and perform the same if not better. K beauty-esque
