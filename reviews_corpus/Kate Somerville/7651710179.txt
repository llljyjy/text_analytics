I have combo skin but I always try to reach for gel products when it comes to my treatments. The gel formula does a good job absorbing into the skin but it does leave it with a silky feeling afterwards. I love the combination of salicylic acid, niacinamide, and AHAs as I do see a difference when I have break outs. I feel like the combination of all three helps clear out the gunk, calm down the redness, and prevent pigmentation from forming. It does have a slight medicinal smell which I’m not the biggest fan of but it’s very faint and only when you’re physically smelling the product. I love the combo of ingredients
