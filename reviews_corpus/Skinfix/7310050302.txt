My rating is purely based on the way it feels on the skin, otherwise it is a fine product. I brought the travel / sample size to try this moisturizer out and glad I did not buy the larger tub. It’s just not for me. Left my skin very greasy feeling, almost like rubbing a balm all over my face. If you have extremely dry skin this might work and you might like it but I have normal-to-dry acne prone skin and it was a bit too much. Thankfully it didn’t break me out and honestly my skin did feel good the following morning but I just can’t see myself using this in my morning routine under sunscreen. A little greasy...
