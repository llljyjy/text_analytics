This is a super thick moisturizer, and definitely does the trick of soothing the dry areas of skin. But honestly, I would put this on at night and in the morning would wake up feeling really dry. I feel like this is a product that needs to constantly be reapplied. Personally, I prefer using a drop or 2 of facial oil mixed with my every day moisturizer to really wake up with hydrated and happy skin! Not the best
