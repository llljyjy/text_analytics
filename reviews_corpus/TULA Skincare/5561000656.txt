I loved the texture of this cream and found it to be very lightweight. Unfortunately, it wasn’t enough to keep my combination skin moisturized during the colder days we’ve been having. It didn’t seem to do much for my hyperpigmentation either and I found that I was breaking out a bit more than usual when I used it every morning and night. The concept of having great probiotic ingredients is great but this product left me underwhelmed. An average moisturizer
