I received this product free from Influenster in exchange for my honest review. I have the rose eye stick from Tula which I like so I was excited to try this. This product did give me a cooling effect after application but I had to apply quite a bit of product to feel that. Once I applied it my undereyes did brighten up for sure because of a silverish tint that was left. I really had to massage the product into my skin for it to go away. I am East Indian and have olive skin tone which may be why. It may not do the same on a lighter skinned person. I would not buy it again but would recommend it to anyone with lighter skin! Left a tint
