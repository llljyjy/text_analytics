This is definitely a lightweight moisturizer so if you’re someone with dry skin this might not be enough. It absorbs nicely so it would be good for layering. I live in New England and can’t wear this alone during the winter. It feels soft and silky and isn’t heavily perfumed ( a huge pet peeve of mine in facial products) which is a huge plus for me. I use this as a daytime moisturizer during the warmer months. creamy and luxurious
