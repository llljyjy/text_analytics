I received a sample of Pink Cloud with an order and as other reviewers have mentioned, it does smell like stinky feet. As far as the plumping it gives I’d say that’s fairly good. Moisturizing is no better than other drug store brand I’ve used. There was some pilling and flaking when I used it over a serum. My skin is on the dry side so when putting pro’s and con’s together *too high priced* I’d have to take a pass on this in the future. Not for me
