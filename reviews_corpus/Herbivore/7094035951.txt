This stuff STINKS, literally. I get that it’s fragrance free but ew. I’ve tried many other fragrance free products that don’t smell this bad. The only good thing is that it’s not a strong smell. It’s very subtle but I could pick up on it even before I applied it and I can still smell it on my hands after washing. Also, it says light-weight but eh, no, not for me. I had to DRAG it across my face to apply it. I went back in the jar to get more just to circle and pat in the spots I couldn’t move the product to, and I already had a decent amount to begin with. Most moisturizers you don’t need a lot. Glad I tried the tiny trial size before buying the full size. I love Herbivore, but…
