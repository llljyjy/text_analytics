The Foreo Bear is a modern microcurrent, facial toning device with 5 levels of intensity. This device includes a USB charger cable, stand, travel pouch, sample serum, and manual. It connects via Bluetooth to sync with the FOREO app for a customized routine or can be used manually as well. The app guides you easily through your routine. The treatments target fine lines and wrinkles while firming the skin as well. Skin looks plumper, more lifted, and refreshed immediately after one use. nan
