Takes my makeup off, but still not for me. I’m quite new to makeup removing balms and oils. I thought I would love this as a double cleanser, but I haven’t been reaching it for it. I typically find myself using a good amount to really get all my makeup off. The texture isn’t for me, and doesn’t leave my face feeling any different. The smell is wonderful and fruity and a lot of product does come in the jar. I just wouldn’t buy this again. Does the job
