My mom’s side of the family has a history of developing pigmentation almost like freckles (which is uncommon for Filipinos) which eventually develops into liver spots as they age so I decided to use niacinamide to combat this while in my 30s. Within a few months I saw a visible difference in my skin: it’s more even, less oily and the freckles seem to be less prominent. Will definitely be a permanent fixture in my skincare routine.
