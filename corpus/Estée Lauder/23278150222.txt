This eye cream is very light and smooth. It doesn’t feel tacky, sticky or heavy at all. The texture is very silky and it doesn’t have an unpleasant scent. I didn’t notice a lot of improvement (I don’t really have a lot of fine lines around my eyes), but with consistent daily use, I did see a little improvement.
