I found this moisturizer to be very thick and heavy. It might be good for someone with severely dry skin, but I found it too be too heavy for me even for cold, dry winter days. It does do a good job at moisturizing, but as someone who’s skin tends to get oily easily, it was not for me. I’m also not a fan of the scent, I found it a little too “grandma-esque” for someone in their early thirties. Very heavy
