I received a sample size of this product to review. It has a nice light scent and feels nice going on your skin. I did feel like it took a lot of product to cover my face. Meaning I had to dip my finger in a few times to get more product. It’s not just because it was a small sample. I did not notice any visible difference in my skin , but I didn’t expect to with so little to try. It would depend greatly on the cost of the product if I would purchase this. I have a feeling it would too expensive for me to consider a staple in my drawer. It’s nice but…
