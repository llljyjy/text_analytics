I have combo oily skin with dry areas around my nose. I’ve used a lot of face products, especially moisturizers and creams. I LOVED this product for the first few days. It’s very thick, it stays on all night while I sleep. My skin isn’t sensitive, but after a few days my skin did something that hasn’t happened since highschool (I’m 31!)… I broke out. Pretty badly all over my chin, my forehead, my nose. I stopped using this and it went away. I wanted to love this, but it just isn’t a fit for me. It’s a good cream, but…
