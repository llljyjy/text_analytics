This moisturizer is fine but nothing special. The scent is nice, it made my sensitive skin red for a minute or two after use, but then that it faded back to normal, but I felt like it didn’t actually moisturize my skin very well. My skin felt almost the same after using it as it did beforehand, and I’d find myself adding more moisturizer later in the day as my skin got drier. Very underwhelming. Underwhelming
