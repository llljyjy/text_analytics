I bought this along with a couple other sunscreens as I’ve never had much luck finding decent facial SPF, and this one is no better than any drug store brand. It doesn’t have a white cast, but it pills to no end. Very disappointing. I’m also curious about how the reviews on the Sephora site are so good, but as soon as I looked on other sites, everyone was mentioning the pilling. Not a good product for the price. Very overhyped.
