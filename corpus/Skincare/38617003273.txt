I really like this product-- my skin is GLOWING! There is no odor, which I find important in skincare. It is not sticky, nor does it clog my pores. I will say that it comes out of the bottle SUPER fast. The directions on the back say to only pump halfway but sometimes it can be a bit messy. The overall packaging is unique and fun with the twist opening, but I do wish that the product would not come out so fast.  Will definitely be purchasing another one when this runs out. nan
