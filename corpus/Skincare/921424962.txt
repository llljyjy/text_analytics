Rave reviews online and from Sephora employees prompted me to finally buy this. I thought I would love it, but it’s just okay. Pretty darn good. It doesn’t smell at all like peach, which I hoped for. It feels like a silicone-y gel that coats your lips and moisturizes them pretty well. It’s nice, not anything super special. The best part about it is the aesthetic of the product. Like, but don’t love
