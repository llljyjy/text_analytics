This is a nice sunscreen- it absorbs into my skin quickly, doesn’t leave me looking greasy and doesn’t have much if any of a scent.  It doesn’t irritate my skin at all, and didn’t cause any sort of breakouts.  The thing I really dislike about this is how watery is it!  It’s so messy when it comes out of the bottle.  I much prefer a thicker cream for my sunscreen. It’s decent
