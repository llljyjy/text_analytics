This product will not blend in well enough for anyone who has a little bit color to their skin. I don’t wear makeup so I was looking for something to offset some redness on my face. This product might be okay if you’re going to cover it with makeup (I can’t speak to how it sits under makeup), but this is going to leave you looking pale in the face if you are trying to use this by itself and you have even the slightest tan to your skin. Maybe it's okay if you use makeup, but not without
