It’s so hard to say if a cream or any aging marketed product truly works until you’ve used it for a long time. I can not quite pin point the scent of this cream. It may be eucalyptus. It’s not a scent that bothers me but if you are sensitive to that kind of thing you may steer clear. Other than that the cream is nice. It’s not greasy or heavy at all. I do use a lose strength retinol a couple times a week and I did notice a slight burning when putting the cream after it. But nothing that caused irritation or reactions and only lasted a second. I love that I was sent a good size jar so I will definitely continue to use it up. Confidence in a Cream
