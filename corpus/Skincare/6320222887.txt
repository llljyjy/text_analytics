I had used the water cream and loved it! I have drier skin so I thought this would be even better and it would be worth a shot. This is definitely more moisturizing than the water cream, but it is substantially heavier. It also didn’t seem to sink into my skin as well… by the end of the day I was oily and greasy. That said, I do think it works well to moisturize. Rather than returning, I decided to keep this for nighttime use and to just re-up on the water cream for daytime use.
