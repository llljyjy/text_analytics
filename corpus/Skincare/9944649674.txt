It’s always nice to get to try a new product that is typically out of my budget. However, this one fell a bit below the mark. From first glance, the packaging screams “higher end,” but that’s all that does. The way you have to release the powder into the liquid and then shake…there is so much product remaining in the piece you throw away. But I gave it the benefit of the doubt and tried it for a couple weeks. I saw no difference in my skin with regular use. Personally, I wouldn’t purchase this for myself or a friend. Not sure how to make it better either. It just missed the mark. Ok…I guess
