These are really too small to be usable. I was eager to try the new formulation of the vitamin C given their last formulation would oxidize so fast, but I’ll just pass and find another brand. When I opened the powder to mix in the liquid, the powder exploded out and I lost quite a bit. Either the vitamin C or the hydration serum did make me breakout. I won’t be able to use them long enough given the small sample sizes to see if this is a temporary purge or a legit do not use this product. I’ve never had breakouts with other vitamin C products. The vitamin C is very sticky as others have mentioned. I also suspect because of the weird tiny packaging that it will be very hard to get the last of the product out so a lot will be wasted. I’m in Canada so it is harder to get some of the more popular US brands. For the price, I expected better
