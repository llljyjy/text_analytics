I’ve noticed my skin looking a little dull over the last couple months, so I was excited to give this a try! The formula is pretty loose so it can be runnier than other serums I’ve tried, but it absorbs quickly and melds very well with whatever I’ve layered on top - moisturizer, spf, foundation, etc. I haven’t noticed huuuuge results, but my skin feels like it has more elasticity and is less dull!
