For an eye cream this does a nice job of hydrating the under eye area. It absorbed into my skin nicely. There wasn’t a smell or oiliness to the product. Didn’t cause any irritation to my eyes. I didn’t find it to brighten like it claims. So if you are looking to help out dark circles this isn’t the product for you. But if you want a nice Hydrating quick absorbing eye cream that will leave your undereyes looking like you have proper water intake then this is it! Hydrating eye cream
