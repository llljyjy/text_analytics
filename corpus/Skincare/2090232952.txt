Estee Lauder Revitalizing Supreme+ Youth Power Creme is very rich and creamy like a body lotion. It doesn’t feels too heavy. It is lightly scented, but the scent dissipates quickly. My skin definitely felt and appeared much more hydrated after using it. Per the product details on the Sephora website, it is good for all skin types and for addressing “fine lines and wrinkles, dryness, and loss of firmness and elasticity“. Based on my short-term use, I would say that it is great for dryness. Longer term use would be necessary to adequately review the other benefit claims. It's decent
