Since I adored every other Kiehl’s Midnight Recovery product I’d tried (eye cream, facial oil), I thought I’d love this cleansing oil. Unfortunately, it’s nothing special and not overly cleansing.  It’s very light and I feel as if my hands and skin need to be perfectly dry to get the most from it.  If you’re looking for a very light oil cleanser, then this may be for you but I’ve had better.  It’s not offensive.  Just not overly effective or satisfying. For light cleansing
