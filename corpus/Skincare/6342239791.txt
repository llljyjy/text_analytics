I’ve been a long time user of Simple makeup wipes until they recently changed their formula. I was on the hunt for another brand of makeup wipes that wouldn’t dry my skin out or make my eyes red, but was having trouble finding something. I’ve always loved Clinique so I thought I’d give this a try and I’m very pleased! This is gentle, fragrance free, and my skin does not feel dry or irritated afterwards. I bought this mini version because I wasn’t sure I would like it, but I will be buying the full sized once I run out. Gentle and effective
