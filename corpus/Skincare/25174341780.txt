I received a free sample of this product from Bzz Agent. I was looking forward to trying it because I already use Kiehl’s Avocado Eye Cream and love it. Unfortunately, I did not see many improvements with its use. I liked the moisturizing benefit but found that it didn’t absorb well and smeared when makeup was applied over it. I will continue to use it at night and the avocado cream during the day under makeup. I typically like Kiehl’s products and hope others see benefits from this product but I will stick with my favorite avocado eye cream. I really wanted to love this product but…
