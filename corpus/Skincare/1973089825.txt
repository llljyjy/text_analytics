I received the Estee Lauder Advance Night Repair Eye Supercharged Gel-Creme complimentary of Influenster for review purposes. All statements are my honest opinions & experiences. According to Estee Lauder the Estee Lauder Advance Night Repair Eye Supercharged Gel-Creme is suppose to moisturize, brighten dark circles, and reduce the look of fine lines. The eye gel-creme is definitely moisturizing however, I did not notice a difference in my fine lines or dark circle. Therefore, I would not recommend this eye gel-creme for individuals who need advance fine line and or dark circle repair. Moisturizing
