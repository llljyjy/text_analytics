I wanted to love this product due to the ease in using it, but the scent was overpowering—if you have allergies, you will want to avoid this product. Additionally, I didn’t feel like the spray was going to be effective without rubbing it in to ensure full coverage which eliminated the hoped for spray-and-go use scenario. I wanted to be lazy and just spray on SPF, but it turns out that sticking with a cream SPF is still the way to go for me. Overpowering Scent
