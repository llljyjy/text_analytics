The packaging is nothing special. Mine had a sticker placed over the original printing on the bottle, so I’m not sure if there was a printing error. However, it’s not what’s on the outside that counts, so let’s talk about the product itself.  The formula is lightweight and barely scented. It is also low sud and creamy. I love how the light, creamy texture feels upon my skin. I’ve been able to effectively use it for a week but haven’t seen a difference as yet, due to the short time frame. I can say that my skin feels drier after each use. The dryness isn’t unpleasant, but I do which it had more moisturizing qualities about it. My skin is oily. Overall, I like the product, and plan to continue to use it and document any noticeable changes in my skin. It’s good
