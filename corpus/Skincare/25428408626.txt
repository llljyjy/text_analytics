I am so picky when it comes to sunscreens and it seems like every sunscreen I’ve tried (from low end to high end sunscreens) have always made me feel super oily and sticky. I was hesitant to buy this at first because of the price and it was an oil, which I have never used a face oil but I love it. I found out 1-2 drops after moisturizing works the best for me. It doesn’t make me feel oily throughout the day and this is my favorite sunscreen I’ve ever tried. Although, when this came in the mail the top was cracked and half the bottle had spilled. This very well could have been to freezing and thawing since I live in the Midwest. But I was disappointed since I love it so much. love this sunscreen for my combo skin except…
