I bought this as traveling set and started using this on my travel instead of my regular night cream. Loved it! It’s like an Uber thin mask and you can just wash your face normally when you wake up in the morning. After washing my face, I apply my serum and then this mask. For folks who are saying it’s too wet, you need to apply a thin layer (don’t take too much if you feel your skin is not absorbing it) and let it dry for a few seconds. For me it dried off right away and my skin started glowing in the morning! All the travel, sun exposure and stress takes a toll on your face. This was definitely a good addition to my skincare routine in that time.
