I have sensitive skin which is both oily and dehydrated. I use this as a night cream after applying my essence and serum(s). I really enjoy the smell of this cream and the consistency is satisfying. However, I have tried using this on its own when my skin felt irritated and did not experience a reduction of redness or irritation. It is a beautifully formulated cream and I love the experience of using it, but the only benefit I really feel from it is hydration. its nice
