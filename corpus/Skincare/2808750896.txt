I bought the spray back when Sephora first started carrying it, and was immediately hooked. The serum brought overnight release to the redness and sensitivity on my cheeks, and it gets five stars for effectiveness. That said, I’m taking away a star because as others have noted, the dispensing mechanism is rather harsh. It’s impossible to control how much comes out at once, and I feel like product is being wasted. If that issue were fixed, the serum would be perfect. Overnight effectiveness
