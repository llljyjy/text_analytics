Beautiful gorgeous fabulous packaging in true Merit fashion, however, the consistency is very watery and you must shake the bottle before pumping out since the ingredients separate (which they write to do in the instructions). A lot of my skincare already has niacinamide already included so I don’t feel it was a super necessary ingredient for them to add, but I always love a hyaluronic acid as I have very dry skin and am a tretinoin user—and the HA in this serum seemed to get the job done. This serum was completely odorless so I felt safe using it on my extremely sensitive skin. simple hydrating serum in gorgeous packaging
