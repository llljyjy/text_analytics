This is a great serum for those with dry, mature, and/or textured skin. Ideal for those looking for something to soften their skin. It’s quite pricey however and I don’t think it’s a must have serum that most people need in their routine. If your moisturizer isn’t cutting it, this could be a nice boost for your skin. Otherwise, I have other serums I prefer to splurge on. Like it but not a must have
