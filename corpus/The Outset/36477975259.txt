I’ve tried several serums, most of which I like, but this one hands down has the prettiest bottle. This serum is lighter than others I’ve tried and it works well for me, especially since it’s now summer where I live. For someone with drier skin than mine maybe it would be fine followed by a rich moisturizer.It is easy to use just a drop or 2. That’s enough to cover my face (and if I use 2 drops my neck).
