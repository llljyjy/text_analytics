The Biossance Squalane +Omega Repair Cream was not for me. While it was very hydrating, I found it to be too heavy and greasy. I tried using it under my makeup and it caused my foundation to just melt off. I would say this for very very dry skin or for a specific drying event such as following a big chemical peel or sunburn. I personally won’t use it daily because it’s just too much for me. Very intense
