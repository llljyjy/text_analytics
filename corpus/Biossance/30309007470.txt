This is a GREAT moisturizer and I love the product. However, do I think it’s worth the price? Absolutely not. This is way too much money to spend on a moisturizer, especially considering the small size. I got this in a skincare Sephora set but I wouldn’t purchase it for itself. Also, it’s hard to get past the smell. It smells absolutely horrible. Sad because this is a great moisturizer, but you can get similar moisturizers for a fraction of the price, no need to spend this much money. Amazing, but…
