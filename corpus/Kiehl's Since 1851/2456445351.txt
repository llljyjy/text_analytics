The Kiehl’s Powerful-Strength Dark Circle Reducing Vitamin C Serum has a thick consistency that blends into the eye area. I don’t really have dark circles, so that part really did nothing for me, but I do have wrinkles under my eyes that I HATE. I did notice after a while that the serum dried out my under eye, resulting in dried crease lines flaking, so I just made sure to moisturize after putting on the serum. My only reservations are its tendency to dry out my undereye, and honestly I didn’t really see much results of my wrinkles lessening. I was gifted the product in exchange for my review. It was ok
