I was really interested in this product due to having dark circles around my eyes. Having used this product for about a month now, I’m sad to say I’ve seen no visible brightening in my under-eye circles, no reduction in the bags under my eyes, and the only benefit was its a good facial beauty cream that reduces wrinkles and lines and keeps skin looking great. As far as mainly a eye cream it didn’t work much for me but your mileage may vary. Kind of Worked...
