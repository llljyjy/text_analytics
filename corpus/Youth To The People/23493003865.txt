I really, really wanted to like this cleanser. I got the travel sized and realized it was irritating my skin everytime I used it (I have rosacea, sensitive skin). A real bummer. I liked that they had a value size and they encouraged reusing the same bottle, the lather was nice, the scent (if you like that type of thing) could only be described as clean veggies.Sensitive skin peeps just be cautious before buying, my rosacea was not a fan. Wasn’t for me— sensitive skin peeps be aware
