I received this product from Influenster to provide my honest opinion. To start I was only given a sample size so I am not able to provide any information on long term use. This product has no odour and came in a gel formula. It did provide ample moisture as advertised. However, I found I had a large increase in breakouts with this product. Due to this fact I would not recommend this to any one prone to breakouts. Not good for acne prone skin
