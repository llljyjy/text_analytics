Admittedly I haven’t used this to remove makeup, which I think it may do well with. However, on bare skin I would not reach for this product. It cleanses decently, but leaves an oily-feeling film on the skin. It is advertised to remove makeup, but if I were to use it as so I would definitely be double-cleansing with a different cleanser after using this. As a cleanser alone, this product isn’t great. I gave it 3 stars though, because despite the oily feeling after use, it wasn’t an oil that clogged pores or caused breakouts. Leaves an oily film
