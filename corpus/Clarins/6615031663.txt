I received a free trial 7 day pack of this serum, but this review is my honest opinion. While 7 days isn’t enough to see much change, I really enjoyed how this hydrates my undereye area. Hydration alone isn’t enough to justify the cost of this product though. My skin is slightly sensitive and this didn’t irritate it at all. This product is scented in case that’s a concern for you, but I enjoyed the scent. I don’t think it did anything else for my under eyes except hydration. Hydrating
