This is such an interesting eye cream with the two different formulas. There is a gel and a concentrate that mix together to form a watery gel that applies easily. I use my ring fingers to gently pat the gel in as part of my nightly routine. I use eye creams and serums mostly as a preventative measure and over the past few weeks I haven’t noticed any develop so it’s doing the job! The only downsides to this are the price and smell, if you’re sensitive then definitely try a sample before splurging on a whole bottle. An innovative serum gel
