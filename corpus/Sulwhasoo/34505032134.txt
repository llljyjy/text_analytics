I am disappointed to say that this “firming cream“ is not worth the money. I was gifted this product by Sulwhasoo. I hate leaving bad reviews, but I would hate even more for someone to waste their money. I applied the cream and instantly noticed a placebo effect: my skin was instantly shiny but still the exact same texture. The next morning it was back to its normal self. I feel terrible that I have to leave a review like this, but the cream is very overpriced. Not Worth the Price At All
