This is an OK retinol serum. I think it’s best for those who are new to retinol and those with sensitive skin. The serum helped smooth texture on my skin and did well with managing oil in my oilier areas. It did nothing for any hyperpigmentation. It does not create fragrance, which is a plus. The natural scent reminds me of the Drunk Elephant retinol serum, which I’ve used with great results. Overall, There are more effective OTC retinol treatments on the market, both at and below this product’s price point. It’s meh
