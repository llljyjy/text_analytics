I’ve used Confidence in a Cream in the past, and while this updated “supercharged“ version is very similar to its predecessor, it does have a few differences. This Confidence feels both more hydrating and lighter, but it still wouldn’t be my go-to for a summer moisturizer. Like the original, it is thick and creamy without too much of a smell. The retinol content is low enough for my somewhat sensitive skin to handle on an every other day basis, but for those with very sensitive skin, I would be inclined to give it a pass. This newer version did not seem to be too heavy to break me out like the previous iteration, but that may be different for everyone.
