I was excited to try the IT Cosmetics Bye Bye Makeup: 3-in-1 Makeup Melting Cleansing Balm but was let down when I found out the product cant be used on the eyes.  The day I used it I had cat eyeliner and mascara on which was the whole reason I was trying it - to see how well it could remove eye makeup - so to learn that the eyes were not a safe zone was disappointing.  Nevertheless, I did use it and it did remove the little bit of concealer I wore but I cannot justify purchasing it on my own. Wanted to love it but...
