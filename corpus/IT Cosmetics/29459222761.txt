This hydrating and anti-aging cream did not hydrate and did not make my skin look younger. It was just eh. It had fragrance which isn’t unpleasant but I didn’t like it. I don’t typically use any products on my face that have fragrance. In terms of helping my skin feel moisturized it didn’t do better than what I typically use which is an over the counter regular face cream. If anything I felt like it dried out my skin. As for its anti aging properties I didn’t see any improvement. I wouldn’t recommend nan
