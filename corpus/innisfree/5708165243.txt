I bought this with hopes that it would control the oil on my face underneath my makeup on my wedding day as I would have to wear this for at least 8 hours. I did a few test runs and it did exactly what I wanted it to do. My face is very oily so when I put this moisturizer on it definitely makes my skin feel tighter and clean. The reviews that state that this makes their skin feel way too tight might have sensitive skin that doesn’t respond well to this product. Mine however absolutely loves it. If you have extremely oily skin that isn’t sensitive then definitely try this out. This is now a staple
