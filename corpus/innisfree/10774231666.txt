I was super excited when I finally got to try this, but the ingredient list makes no sense for a hydrating product. Alcohol Denat is the fourth ingredient, which dries out your skin... why is this in in a HYDRATING serum? When I use this, it feels nice on my skin and smells okay, but it does not give me a long lasting hydrating due to its contradicting ingredients. I would not recommend this for dry skin at all, it will dehydrate your skin and dry it out even more. Alcohol Denat is a drying ingredient
