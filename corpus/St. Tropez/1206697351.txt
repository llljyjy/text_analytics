As far as sunless tanning moisturizers go this worked well.  Skin felt smooth and soft after use and glowed with a subtle tan.  However, the watermelon scent wears off almost immediately after application and soon after turns to your usual potent sunless tanner odor.  If this smell doesn’t bother you, than this is an excellent product, but if you’re like me and don’t want to smell, let alone smell like, sunless tanner, know what you’re getting into.
