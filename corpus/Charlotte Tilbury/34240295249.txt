I like that Charlotte Tilbury’s Magic cream contains beneficial ingredients such as Hyaluronic acid, vitamins C and E, bionymph peptide complex and more. It’s thick and creamy, so it doesn’t take a lot. The scent is pleasant and not over powering. It did make my skin feel more hydrated but I didn’t get that radiant glow everyone is raving they got when they used it. (I’ve been using it daily for two weeks) I guess I expected more from all of the hype. I would recommend it for those with super dry skin nan
