When I was sent this La Mer eye cream I was ready to hate on it because $230 for an eye cream is a lot of coins. However, this product is so light weight and a little really does go a long way. This product feels great and the packaging gives off bougie vibes. I still can’t 100% say I’d tell anyone to spend all their coins on eye cream in this economy but if you got the money I say go for it. Feels like the Price
