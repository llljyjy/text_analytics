These types of layering products have become an important step in my dry skin routine. I use it as an “essence” step & it really helps hydrate my skin & prep for my serum. The texture is like a thin watery gel & it feels great! My only complaint (aside from price) is the added fragrance. Doh! So many amazing ingredients but artificial fragrance just isn’t one of them. I wish high-end brands would at least switch to natural fragrance which is better for your skin. Unless you’re rich & you LOVE perfume in your skincare, I would probably recommend the Origins Mega Mushroom Treatment Lotion instead. Almost, but not quite
