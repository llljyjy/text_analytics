If you’re looking for good value in a moisturizing mask, this is worth a try. It’s thick, creamy and soothing. The grapefruit scent is light and doesn’t smell super artificial. It noticeably moisturizes, but I haven’t seen any other effects. I have oily, acne prone skin and it doesn’t break me out, even if I leave some of the extra product on my skin, which is a win! I find myself reaching for it when my skin is feeling dry, even with a drawer full of other options. Moisturizing and doesn’t break me out
