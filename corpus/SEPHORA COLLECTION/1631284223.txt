If you’re like me and were hoping this would be similar to the discontinued Peptide day firming cream, you’ll be disappointed. This formula is thick, chunky, and just sits on the skin. It has a filmy, matte finish. It also broke me out all over my face. No firming effect at all. Would make a fine hand cream, but I’m looking forward to returning it this weekend. Sephora needs to stop chasing “trending” skincare and keep formulas that actually work. Disappointing and chunky
