Decent hydrating moisturizer but nothing special and doesn’t deliver on promises. According to What’s In My Jar, the concentration of peptides is too low to be effective, so if that’s what you’re looking for it’s not worth it. I liked the texture and it was hydrating so it did it’s job as a regular moisturizer, but you can find something simple at the drugstore for much less if all you want is a regular moisturizer. Don’t regret buying it — I liked the texture and it hydrated my skin — but wouldn’t recommend or purchase again. I’ll stick with the ordinary’s moisturizer for now.
