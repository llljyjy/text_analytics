Don’t let the incentivized reviews fool you, this is not the wonder product the ratings would suggest. I’ve tried many other AHA products before and they’ve all seemed to have a spectrum of different effects for me. This is the first time I’ve tried one and it’s done absolutely nothing. And I really mean that, my skin looks exactly the same as before I used the product. I’ve used face cleansers that are more effective. This product does come in a very fancy, impressive looking box. But I’m getting the feeling that you’re paying more for the branding and marketing than the product itself. I’ve already packed mine up for a return and am unlikely to try out any of the other Ranavat products. An Expensive Placebo
