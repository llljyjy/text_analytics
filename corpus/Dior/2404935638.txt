The texture is amazing and I felt it absorbed into the skin well but brands like Dior need to use less fragrance in skincare products. While the smell does not linger, it does irritate both my skin and my nose since it’s such a pronounced scent. I also noticed some breakouts after a week of use and I’m sure it’s due to the fragrance. I really wanted to test the 30 day claim but it’s a no for me and my sensitive skin. Too much fragrance …
