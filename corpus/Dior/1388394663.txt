A well balanced cleanser. I have been using it on its own and with a cleansing brush and it does not dry me out or leave me flakey.  But it still leaves me feeling clean and not greasy- a hard balance to strike with my combination skin.  It is a luscious mousse formula with a mild scent. As a bonus, the bottle is huge and the packaging design is attractive with a clean and simple look. Creamy and gentle
