I’ve been using this for about a week and haven’t noticed much of a difference thus far. My skin has had a bit more a “glow” to it but I’m unsure if it’s due to this product or not. I do enjoy the application of the product; however, I agree with a previous reviewer in that it would be better dispensed through a dropper as opposed to a pump. The consistency of the product is similar to a toner rather than a serum in my opinion. Feels like a toner
